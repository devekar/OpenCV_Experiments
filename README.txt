Experimentation with OpenCV
===========================

Note: CvBlob library required.


1. MouseSimulation.cpp
Simulate mouse actions by tracking colored objects; blue for tracking cursor location, presence of yellow blob for left click and red for right click.


2. Flick_PinchSimulation.cpp
Use of flick to change picture and pinch gesture for zoom in-out and moving image.
Blue for flick, blue and yellow for pinch gesture, only yellow to drag image across screen. Blue also tracks cursor position.
